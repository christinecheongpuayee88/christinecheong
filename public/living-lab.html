What is Living Lab

Where enterprise problems
meet academic rigour

The NUS-ISS Living Lab is a structured co-innovation programme where organisations bring their most pressing technology and business challenges to NUS-ISS. Our faculty researchers and graduate students then collaborate directly with your team — using cutting-edge methodologies and tools — to develop, test, and implement real solutions.

Unlike traditional consulting, Living Lab projects are grounded in rigorous academic research while remaining commercially focused. You get fresh thinking, specialised expertise, and a dedicated team — all aligned to your business outcomes.

🔬
Applied Research

Every project is anchored in evidence-based methodologies, ensuring solutions are both innovative and robust.

💼
Commercial Focus

We measure success by business outcomes: efficiency gains, cost reductions, revenue growth, and competitive advantage.

🤝
True Co-creation

Your team is embedded in the project — not just an end-user. This ensures solutions fit your context and your people adopt them.

📜
Clear IP Arrangements

Transparent intellectual property agreements are established upfront so both parties benefit fairly from the work.

The Process

From challenge to delivered solution

A structured three-phase model designed for pace, clarity, and commercial impact — typically spanning 12 to 24 weeks.

01
📋 Define

Scoping & Alignment

We work closely with your leadership to define the challenge, success metrics, data requirements, and project boundaries. A living lab project director is assigned to your account from day one.

  • Challenge discovery workshop
  • Feasibility & data assessment
  • Project charter sign-off
  • Team formation & role assignments
02
⚙️ Build

Research & Development

Our interdisciplinary team of faculty researchers, data scientists, and engineers develops, tests, and iterates on solutions in close collaboration with your stakeholders through structured sprint cycles.

  • Agile fortnightly sprint reviews
  • Prototype build & user testing
  • Regular steering committee updates
  • Benchmarking against industry standards
03
🚀 Deliver

Deployment & Knowledge Transfer

Solutions are deployed in your environment and your team is fully equipped to own and evolve the outcome independently. We provide documentation, training, and a post-project support period.

  • Production deployment & integration
  • Staff upskilling & workshops
  • Full technical documentation
  • 30-day post-launch advisory support

Featured Project × NUS-ISS

Analytics across Service, Product & Process

Three major problem domains identified in partnership with a leading FMCG enterprise in Asia Pacific — each addressed through applied analytics, predictive modelling, and data-driven strategy.

🙋
Service Analytics

Customer & Stakeholder Engagement

Analytics that directly serve consumers, marketing teams, and business stakeholders — delivering value through data-driven touchpoints across campaigns and CRM.

  • Consumer Needs Assessment Mapping AI productivity opportunities for employees across APAC markets
  • Business Problem Definition Translating RGM and supply chain challenges into analytics use cases with data PMs
  • Digital Campaign Activation Designing and executing consumer acquisition funnels across paid and owned channels
  • CRM Planning & Customer Engagement Supporting CRM execution with data accuracy, segmentation, and campaign readiness
Interns: Yang Yaming  ·  Nicole Khoo  ·  Jaikishen S.N.  ·  Zhao Xiangkun
📦
Product Analytics

Predictive Models & Data Products

Building tangible analytical artefacts — predictive models, dashboards, and data products that generate actionable intelligence for the enterprise partner's marketing, CRM, and operations teams.

  • Propensity & Conversion Modelling Predict user conversion likelihood across channels; enable lookalike targeting
  • Customer Churn Prediction Model Identify at-risk consumers to trigger CRM retention interventions
  • Dormant Consumer Reactivation Model Score inactive customers for prioritised CRM messaging allocation
  • Conversion & Revenue Forecasting Forecast campaign CVR and revenue outcomes to guide budget planning
  • Power BI Campaign Dashboards Real-time performance tracking and insight reporting for stakeholders
  • RFM & Demand Forecasting Models Time series and behavioural segmentation for supply chain and customer analytics
Interns: Jin Yifan  ·  Nicole Khoo  ·  Jaikishen S.N.  ·  Zhao Xiangkun
⚙️
Process Analytics

Operations & Workflow Optimisation

Using analytics to streamline internal marketing, supply chain, and technology processes — improving efficiency, quality, and scalability across APAC operations.

  • Revenue Growth Management (RGM) Promo effectiveness modelling and pricing elasticity analysis
  • Supply Chain Optimisation Demand forecasting and inventory management using ML algorithms
  • Martech Operations & Integration Operating CRM, marketing automation, CMS and analytics platforms
  • AI Adoption & Change Management Driving GenAI tool adoption through structured communication and rollout plans
  • Attribution & Media Performance Channel attribution analysis to optimise media spend ROI across campaigns
  • UAT Coordination & QA Test case development and resolution tracking for AI product quality assurance
